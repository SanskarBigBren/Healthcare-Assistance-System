LIVER DISEASE PREDICTION




1. Age: The age of the patient in years.


2. Gender: The gender of the patient (1 = male; 2 = female).


3. Total Bilirubin: The total amount of bilirubin in the patient's blood (in mg/dL).


4. Direct Bilirubin: The amount of direct bilirubin in the patient's blood (in mg/dL).


5. Alkaline Phosphatase: The amount of alkaline phosphatase in the patient's blood (in IU/L).


6. Alamine Aminotransferase (ALT): The amount of ALT in the patient's blood (in IU/L).


7. Aspartate Aminotransferase (AST): The amount of AST in the patient's blood (in IU/L).


8. Total Proteins: The total amount of protein in the patient's blood (in g/dL).


9. Albumin: The amount of albumin in the patient's blood (in g/dL).


10. Albumin and Globulin Ratio: The ratio of albumin to globulin in the patient's blood.
