ALLERGY MEDICATION INFORMATION



AGE (Children)

=> Age group of 2-12 Years


What To Do?

=> Antihistamines: There are many different types of antihistamines that can be used for children in this age group. Some examples include loratadine, cetirizine, and fexofenadine.

=> Nasal corticosteroids: These are nasal sprays that can help reduce inflammation and swelling in the nasal passages. Some examples include fluticasone and mometasone.

=> Leukotriene inhibitors: These are oral medications that can help prevent symptoms caused by certain allergens. Examples include montelukast and zafirlukast.



*It is important to note that you should always talk to a doctor or pharmacist before starting any new medication, especially if you have any underlying health conditions or are taking other medications*.





POINTS TO REMEMBER!


1. It is important to note that you should always talk to a doctor or pharmacist before starting any new medication, especially if you have any underlying health conditions or are taking other medications.

2. There are many different types of allergy medications available, and the appropriate medication and dosage will depend on the individual's age and specific allergy symptoms.
