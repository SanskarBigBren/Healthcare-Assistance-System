DIABETES DISEASE PREDICTION




1. Pregnancies: Number of times the patient has been pregnant.


2. Glucose: Plasma glucose concentration over 2 hours in an oral glucose tolerance test.


3. Blood Pressure: Diastolic blood pressure (mm Hg).


4. Skin Thickness: Triceps skin fold thickness (mm).


5. Insulin: 2-Hour serum insulin (mu U/ml).


6. BMI: Body mass index, which is a measure of body fat based on height and weight (kg/mÂ²).


7. Diabetes Pedigree Function: A function that measures the likelihood of diabetes based on family history.


8. Age: The age of the patient in years.
